About me

My name is Keaton Kraiger and I am currently a senior at Portland State University majoring in computer science. I am working as a grader and Technical course student specialist for Karla Fant's courses in the CS department.

I am also doing research with Dr. Dan Hammerstrom in the Biologically-Inspired Computing Lab, part of Portland State's Center for Brian Inspired Computing division. I am researching biologically inspired computer vision algorithms for object detection and recognition. I am broadly interesting in artificial intelligence and its applications in computer vision and natural language processing.
